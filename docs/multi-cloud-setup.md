# Multi-Cloud Account Setup Guides

This document provides step-by-step guides for setting up free tier accounts on AWS, Azure, and GCP.

## AWS Free Tier Setup

1. Go to [aws.amazon.com/free](https://aws.amazon.com/free) and click "Create a free account".
2. Enter your email and choose a password.
3. Provide your contact information and payment method (you won't be charged for free tier usage).
4. Verify your identity via phone.
5. Choose a support plan (free tier includes basic support).
6. Sign in to the AWS Management Console.

Free tier includes:
- 750 hours of EC2 t2.micro instances
- 5GB S3 storage
- Basic support

## Azure Free Tier Setup

1. Go to [azure.microsoft.com/free](https://azure.microsoft.com/free) and click "Start free".
2. Sign in with a Microsoft account or create one.
3. Provide your contact information and payment method (you won't be charged for free tier usage).
4. Verify your identity via phone or credit card.
5. Sign in to the Azure Portal.

Free tier includes:
- 750 hours of B1S VMs
- 5GB Blob storage
- Basic support

## GCP Free Tier Setup

1. Go to [cloud.google.com/free](https://cloud.google.com/free) and click "Get started for free".
2. Sign in with a Google account or create one.
3. Provide your contact information and payment method (you won't be charged for free tier usage).
4. Verify your identity.
5. Sign in to the Google Cloud Console.

Free tier includes:
- Always Free: f1-micro instances (limited), 5GB storage, etc.
- $300 credit for 90 days.

Note: Free tiers have limitations and may require credit card verification.
